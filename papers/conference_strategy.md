## Journals — Candidates (Ranked by Fit)

### Tier 1: Best fit

1. **Cognitive Systems Research** (Elsevier)
   - THE journal for cognitive architectures (ACT-R, SOAR, CLARION publish here)
   - Path-of-thought as cognitive architecture is squarely in scope
   - Impact factor: ~2.1

2. **Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research (JAIR)**
   - Open access, no publication fees
   - Accepts novel knowledge representation approaches
   - Rigorous but fair review
   - Impact factor: ~5.0

3. **Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence**
   - Open access, faster review cycle
   - Good for interdisciplinary work
   - Impact factor: ~3.0

### Tier 2: Solid alternatives

4. **Knowledge-Based Systems** (Elsevier)
   - Knowledge representation and reasoning systems
   - Impact factor: ~8.0

5. **PeerJ Computer Science**
   - Fast review, open access
   - Fair to independent researchers
   - Impact factor: ~3.5

6. **Artificial Intelligence** (Elsevier)
   - Top journal in the field — prestige play
   - Very competitive
   - Impact factor: ~14.0

### Avoid

- PLOS ONE — too broad, paper gets lost
- ML-focused venues (NeurIPS, ICML journals) — reviewer bias toward transformer/neural net paradigm
