# Ch10 round 2 — meiosis region
mules have evolutionary fitness of zero
mules cannot produce offspring because they cannot perform meiosis
meiosis has two rounds of cell division
mitosis has one round of cell division
meiosis I separates homologous chromosomes
meiosis II separates sister chromatids
DNA replication occurs before meiosis I only
DNA replication does not occur between meiosis I and meiosis II
