# six ALU primitives (add, subtract, multiply, divide, abs, negate).
#
# Teach with:
#   .venv/bin/python benchmarks/batch_teach.py \
#       --db <brain.db> --file benchmarks/curriculum_math.txt
#
# Every line is a fact. Composite operations defined here are later
# executed by MathCompute.execute() walking the graph's stated rule.
# The curriculum mirrors how a child is taught elementary arithmetic:
# the primitive is trusted; the composition is what is learned.

# Halving and doubling — one-step composites of the primitives
to halve a number is to divide the number by two
to double a number is to multiply the number by two
halving reduces a number to half its value
doubling increases a number to twice its value

# Ratio and proportion
a ratio is a division of two numbers
a fraction is a division of numerator by denominator

# Percentage
a percentage is a fraction with denominator of one hundred

# Probability and chance
probability is favorable outcomes divided by total outcomes
chance is the probability of an outcome

# Summation and averages
the sum of numbers is their addition
the mean of a set is the sum divided by the count
the average of a set is the mean of the set

# Magnitude and difference
the difference of two numbers is their subtraction
the magnitude of a number is the absolute value of the number
